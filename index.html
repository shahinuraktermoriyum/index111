President & Founder

Anwar Khan

4 Reviews

Anwar is the Founder and President of OBAT Helpers. He recently retired from his position at General Motors after an almost nineteen year stint with the company as Lead Auditor of Quality Management Systems. He became a Helper in 2004, upon visiting a camp in Bangladesh and learning about the dire situation that the Urdu-Speaking community had faced for decades. Anwar hopes to empower the long forgotten community and help them assimilate into mainstream society as strong, productive members of their communities.Anwar holds a bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering from NED University of Science and Technology,
